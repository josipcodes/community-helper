What?

Love to help out? Or need a hand? Well, that's what we are here for. In order to connect to our fellow neighbors, we have developed a site dedicated to just that - lending a helping hand. It all start from the comfort of your sofa.

How?

Sign up and add your details in a few steps. Simply browse through all of the our open requests and accept those that are to yourr liking. Or create one of your own and wait for one of our helpers to put on a cape.

Why?

Not everyone has friends or family that can help them in their hour of need. In today's fast-paced environment, we tend to forget the power a community has when formed. So, why not help forming it and make a change?
